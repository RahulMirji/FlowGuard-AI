Use this quick-reference sheet during your live pitch to stay aligned with the scoring criteria and hit every key highlight.

## 📊 1. Evaluation & Scoring Breakdown (100 Marks)

*   **Functionality (20 Marks) — "Working Prototype":**
    *   Highlight that this is a fully functional, live-telemetry application.
    *   Show live rain widgets, the interactive planner map, route recommendations, and full-screen navigation.
*   **Innovation (15 Marks) — "Originality":**
    *   Focus on the dual-stakeholder value loop: AI routing for citizens combined with predictive planning/infrastructure desilting reports for municipal bodies.
*   **Technical Implementation (15 Marks) — "Depth & Execution":**
    *   Highlight the technology stack: Google Maps 3D layout, Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite reasoning models, real-time OpenWeather feeds, and Supabase PostgreSQL.
*   **User Experience / UI-UX (10 Marks) — "Design Quality":**
    *   Emphasize visual excellence: Dark-mode blur filters, dynamic hover effects, and modern typography.
*   **Real-world Applicability (15 Marks) — "Usefulness & Scalability":**
    *   Demonstrate immediate deployment capability for Bengaluru during monsoon seasons.
*   **Performance (10 Marks) — "Reliability & Speed":**
    *   Note 100% serverless edge caching (15-min TTL) and local fallback routing mechanisms to protect against API rate limits.
*   **Presentation & Demo (15 Marks) — "Clarity & Pitch":**
    *   Present confidently, stick strictly to the 8-10 minutes window, and ensure a stable connection.

## 📈 2. Core Projections & Metrics (The 6 Pillars)

*   **88.2% — Flood Prediction Accuracy:**
    *   Calculated using historical rainfall databases cross-referenced with live precipitation telemetry.
*   **14% — Carbon Emissions Reduction:**
    *   Obtained by routing cars and commuters around active waterlogged areas, directly reducing traffic gridlock and engine idling.
*   **18% — Operational Cost Savings:**
    *   Pre-emptive cleaning alerts identify clogged drains *before* flooding events occur, reducing emergency maintenance overheads.
*   **25% — Response Time Improvement:**
    *   Enables ambulance and emergency crews to bypass bottleneck hotspots using dynamic risk-averse routing.
*   **75% — Resource Utilization Efficiency:**
    *   Municipal desilting teams and mobile water pump units are deployed straight to AI-targeted severe-risk coordinates.
*   **+8% — Sustainability Score (UN SDGs):**
    *   Aligns city transport systems with UN SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities & Communities) and SDG 13 (Climate Action).
